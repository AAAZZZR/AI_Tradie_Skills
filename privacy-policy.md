AI Tradie ("we", "our", "us") operates a Facebook Page and related services. This policy explains how we handle information in connection with our Meta (Facebook) application.

## Information We Collect

Our app accesses the following through the Meta Graph API:
- **Page management data**: to publish and manage posts on our Facebook Page
- **Page engagement data**: to view post insights and interactions

We do **not** collect, store, or process personal data from Facebook users who interact with our Page beyond what Facebook natively provides to Page administrators.

## How We Use Information

Information accessed through the Meta API is used solely to:

- Monitor post performance and engagement
- Manage our Page presence

## Data Storage

We do not store Facebook user data on external servers. All interactions occur through the official Meta Graph API.

## Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties.
